Publication-Ready Self-check:
CHI 2013 Papers & Notes

To help you prepare your final submission, the CHI 2013 committee has created this self-check guide that summarizes requirements for submission. Please see the Preparation Instructions from Sheridan Communications for the official guide, and make your final submission, including your PDF, document source, video preview and supplementary material to PCS.

Review ACM's Copyright Policies

Read the ACM's Copyright and Permissions Policy
Read the ACM's Requirements about 3rd party material
Note: You will be asked to confirm that you agree with these policies on the final submission form.

Copyright notices for your manuscript

LaTeX users- Replace any previous \toappear statements in your source with this one. (i.e., Sheridan's instructions)
Word users - Replace the contents of your copyright block in your doc with this one, formatted as 8pt Times New Roman with CHI 2013 in italics. (see Sheridan's Word preparation Instructions)

Verify Authors' PCS Accounts

Note: All author information for your submission is linked to authors' PCS accounts.
Have each author of your submission do the following:
Verify that his/her full name, including middle initial or name is correct in PCS
Verify that his/her current affiliation is updated and correct in PCS
Verify that his/her affiliation location is correct in PCS

Formatting

Template - Ensure that you have used either the official Word or LaTeX templates provided, or followed the preparation instructions for making the format compliant
Paper size - Verify that your document's paper size is US Letter (8.5x11 in.) - not A4.
Title - Verify that your the First Letter of the Main Words in Your Title are Capitalised
Authors' Line - Verify that all authors' names and affilations match their entries in PCS
Abstract - Authors are encouraged to include an abstract at the beginning of their submission
References - Verify citations are listed in a References section at the end of the document
Author Keywords - Verify that your Author Keywords are included in your document and that the keywords are separated with semicolons.
ACM Classification - Verify that the ACM Classification category you selected is appropriate for your submission and appears in your document.
General Terms - No longer required, but if you wish to include them, verify that they consist of valid ACM General Terms.
Acknowledgements - Ensure to include any funding/special contribution acknowledgements required by any research, financial, or other grants received. (e.g., See example pg 4)
Bad Breaks - Check your submission to avoid bad breaks, widows, and orphans.
Text following headings - Ensure Section and Sub-section heads have at least 2 lines of body text below them when they appear at the end of a page or column.
No page numbers - Ensure there are no numbers at the bottom of pages.
No headers nor footers - Ensure there are no headers or footers at the tops/bottoms of pages.

Images and Figures

B&W appearance - Verify figures are properly legible in black and white.
Resolution - Ensure figures are at least 300x600dpi.
CMYK Color Space - CMYK color space is recommended for figures but not required.
Image file format - Use lossless image formats (e.g., TIFF, PNG) instead of lossy (e.g., JPEG) where possible.
Line width - Verify that all lines in Figures are at least 0.5pt+ thick to ensure visibility

PDF Generation

No Type 3 fonts - Verify no Type 3 fonts are included.
Fonts Embedded - Verify all Type 1 & TrueType fonts are embedded.
PDF settings - Verify all other PDF settings conform to ACM's requirements.

Auxiliary Material

Thumbnail image - (Optional) - You may supply a thumbnail image for your submission (72dpi+, 1in)
Thumbnail image caption - (Mandatory if providing a thumbnail image) - Ensure you have prepared a short caption (20-30 words) to accompany your thumbnail image.
Video figure - (Optional) - If you are providing a video figure, ensure that it conforms to the requirements for accompanying videos.
Auxiliary material - (Optional) - If you are providing a auxiliary material (e.g., data files), ensure you have created a single ZIP file of all material.
Description of Auxiliary material - (Mandatory if providing auxiliary material)) - Ensure you have prepared a short description of the files you are including in your auxiliary material ZIP file.

Video Preview

New for CHI 2013: All submissions must have an accompanying ~30 second "video preview" with your final submission. Check here for more information.  
Video requirements - Verify your Video Preview is in MP4 format and conforms to the requirements listed.
Early Release You will be asked to specify whether or not you agree to the early release (up to 6 weeks prior to CHI 2013) of your video preview for promotional purposes. Be prepared to answer this question.

Additional Final Submission Information

Contribution & Benefits Statement: Ensure you have prepared a short (30 words or less) description of your work for the Conference Program
Summary of Changes: Ensure that you have prepared the list of changes you have made to comply with the Conditional Acceptance requirements of your submission.

After you submit

ACM Copyright Forms and Fixes Needed: Review Sheridan's guide of what to expect after submission, including ACM Copyright Forms and communications regarding fixes needed.
