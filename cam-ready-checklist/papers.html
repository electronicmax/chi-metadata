Pub-Ready Self-check:
CHI 2013 Papers & Notes

To help you prepare your final submission, the CHI 2013 committee has created this self-check guide that summarises requirements prior to submission. Please see the official Preparation Instructions from Sheridan Communications for the official normative guide, and make your final submission, including your PDF, document source, video preview and supplementary material to PCS.

Review ACM's Copyright Policies

Read the ACM's Copyright and Permissions Policy
Read the ACM's Requirements about 3rd party material
You will be asked to confirm that you agree with these policies on the final submission form.

Copyright notices for your manuscript

LaTeX users - Copy & paste this copyright notice into the copyright block of your LaTeX source
Word users - Copy and paste this copyright notice into the Copyright block of your document

Authors' PCS Accounts

Verify that each author's full name, including middle initial is correct in PCS
Verify that each author's current affiliation is updated and correct in PCS
Verify that each author's affiliation location is correct in PCS

New ACM Classification

ACM introduced a new classification system in 2012. Identify the new category appropriate for your submission, and place the appropriate category designation in the block of your submission.

Formatting

Template - Ensure that you have used either the official Word or LaTeX templates provided, or followed the preparation instructions for making the format compliant
Paper size - Verify that your document's paper size is US Letter (8.5x11 in.) - not A4.
Title - Verify that your the First Letter of the Main Words In Your Title Are Capitalised
Authors Line - Verify that all authors' names and affilations match their entries in PCS
Abstract - Authors are encouraged to include an abstract at the beginning of their submission
References - Verify citations are listed in a References section at the end of the document
General Terms - No longer required, but if you wish to keep it, verify that it consists of valid ACM General Terms
Bad Breaks - Check your submission to avoid bad breaks, widows, and orphans.
Text following headings - Ensure Section and Sub-section heads have at least 2 lines of body text below them when they appear at the end of a page or column.widows and orphans.
No page numbers - Ensure there are no numbers at the bottom of pages
No headers nor footers - Ensure there are no headers or footers at the tops/bottoms of pages

Images and Figures

B&W appearance - Verify figures are properly legible in black and white.
Resolution - Ensure figures are at least 300x600dpi.
CMYK Color Space - CMYK color space is recommended for figures but not required.
Image file format - Use lossless image formats (e.g., TIFF, PNG) instead of lossy (e.g., JPEG) where possible.
Line width - Verify that all lines in Figures are at least 0.5pt+ thick to ensure visibility

PDF Generation

No Type 3 fonts - Verify no Type 3 fonts are included.
Type 1 & 2 fonts embedded - Verify all fonts are embedded.
PDF settings - Verify all other PDF settings conform to Sheridan's requirements.

Thumbnail image (optional)

Thumbnail image - Optional: Supply a thumbnail image for your submission (72dpi+, 1in)
Thumbnail caption - If you provided a thubnmail image, prepare a short caption (20-30 words) to go with it.

Video figure (optional)

Video format - Verify that your optional video figure conforms to video requirements for your submission

Video preview (mandatory)

New for CHI 2013: All submissions must have an accompanying ~30 second "video preview" with your final submission. Check here for more information.
Video requirements - Verify your Video Preview conforms to the requirements listed.
Video format - Verify your Video Preview is in mp4 format listed.
