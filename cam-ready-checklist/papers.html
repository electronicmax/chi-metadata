Publication-Ready Self-check:
CHI 2013 Papers & Notes

To help you prepare your final submission, the CHI 2013 committee has created this self-check guide that summarizes requirements for submission. Please see the Preparation Instructions from Sheridan Communications for the official guide, and make your final submission, including your PDF, document source, video preview and supplementary material to PCS.

Review ACM's Copyright Policies

I have read the ACM's Copyright and Permissions Policy
I have read the ACM's Requirements about 3rd party material
Note: You will be asked to confirm that you agree with these policies on the final submission form.

Copyright notices for your manuscript

LaTeX users- Any previous \toappear statements in my source have been replaced with this one. (see Sheridan's instructions)
Word users - I have replaced the contents of my copyright block with the corresponding block in Sheridan's Sample CHI Paper. (see Sheridan's detailed instructions)

Verify Authors' PCS Accounts

Note: All author information for your submission is linked to authors' PCS accounts.
Have each author of your submission do the following:
Verify that his/her full name, including middle initial or name is correct in PCS
Verify that his/her current affiliation is updated and correct in PCS
Verify that his/her affiliation location is correct in PCS

Formatting

LaTeX users - I have obtained and compiled my submission against the latest version of the sigchi class file.
Word users - I have examined the formatting and layout of the official example CHI2013 document and double-checked my submission to ensure that it conforms.
Paper size - My document's paper size is US Letter (8.5x11 in.) - not A4.
Title - The First Letter of the Main Words in My Title are Capitalised
Authors' Line - All authors' names and affilations match their entries in PCS
Abstract - My abstract appears at the beginning of my submission
References - My citations are listed in a References section at the end of my document
Author Keywords - My Author Keywords are included in my document and separated with semicolons.
ACM Classification - The ACM Classification category I have selected is appropriate for my submission and appears in my document.
General Terms - No longer required, but I wish to include them, consist of valid ACM General Terms.
Acknowledgements - Any funding/special contribution acknowledgements required by any research, financial, or other grants received are included. (e.g., See example pg 4)
Bad Breaks - I have checked my submission to avoid bad breaks, widows, and orphans.
Text following headings - I have checked my submission to ensure Section and Sub-section heads have at least 2 lines of body text below them when they appear at the end of a page or column.
No page numbers - I have ensured there are no numbers at the bottom of pages.
No headers nor footers - I have ensure there are no headers or footers at the tops/bottoms of pages.

Images and Figures

B&W appearance - I have verified figures are properly legible in black and white.
Resolution - I have ensured figures are at least 300x600dpi.
CMYK Color Space - (recommended but not required) I have used CMYK color space for Figures when possible.
Image file format - I have used lossless image formats (e.g., TIFF, PNG) instead of lossy (e.g., JPEG) where possible.
Line width - I have verified that all lines in Figures are at least 0.5pt+ thick to ensure visibility.

PDF Generation

No Type 3 fonts - I have verified that no Type 3 fonts are included.
Fonts Embedded - I have verified that all Type 1 & TrueType fonts are embedded.
PDF settings - I have verified that all other PDF settings conform to ACM's requirements.

Auxiliary Material

Thumbnail image - (Optional) - I have (optionally) supplied a thumbnail image and it conforms to the requirements (72dpi+, 1in)
Thumbnail image caption - (Mandatory if providing a thumbnail image) - I have prepared a short caption (20-30 words) to accompany my thumbnail image.
Video figure - (Optional) - I have (optionally) provided a video figure, which conforms to the requirements.
Auxiliary material - (Optional) - I am (optionally) providing auxiliary material (e.g., data files) in a single ZIP file.
Description of Auxiliary material - (Mandatory if providing auxiliary material)) - I have prepared a short description of auxiliary material I am providing.

Video Preview

New for CHI 2013: All submissions must have an accompanying ~30 second "video preview" with your final submission. Check here for more information.  
Video requirements - My Video Preview is in MP4 format and conforms to the requirements.
Early Release I am prepared to answer whether or not I agree to the early release (up to 6 weeks prior to CHI 2013) of my video preview for promotional purposes.
